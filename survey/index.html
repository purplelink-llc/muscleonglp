The GLP-1 muscle survey

There is good trial data on how much lean mass people lose on a GLP-1. There is almost no data on what people actually do about it. We are trying to fix that, and we will publish whatever we find.

Before you start

  • It is completely anonymous. We do not ask for your name or email, and there is nowhere to type free text, so you cannot accidentally send us anything identifying.
  • We do not store your IP address. We keep only a one-way daily hash of it, purely so one person cannot submit fifty times. It cannot be reversed or linked across days.
  • Nothing here is medical advice, and no answer you give changes anything about your care. If a question does not apply, skip it.
  • We will publish the aggregate, including the results that do not flatter us, with the sample size, the dates, and the limitations stated plainly.
1. Which medication are you currently taking?
2. How long have you been on it?
3. Do you currently do resistance training (weights, bands, or bodyweight)?
4. Do you track your protein intake?
5. Roughly how much protein do you eat per day? Optional
6. Has any clinician ever raised muscle loss with you?
7. Have you stopped, or do you plan to stop?
8. If you have stopped, did you notice a change in strength? Optional

What we will do with this

We will publish the aggregate on this site, free for anyone to cite, with the sample size, the collection dates, and how people were recruited. We will state plainly that the sample is self-selected and self-reported, which means it describes the people who answered and not every GLP-1 user in the world.

We will not publish until enough people have responded to describe the result honestly. A survey of forty people presented as a finding is worse than no survey, and it cannot be walked back afterwards.

If you want the results when they land, the weekly research digest is the place we will announce them. That signup is entirely separate from this survey, and the two are never joined up.
