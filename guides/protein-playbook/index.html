Frequently asked

How much protein should I eat on Ozempic or Wegovy?

Research on lean-mass retention during weight loss supports roughly 1.6 to 2.2 grams of protein per kilogram of bodyweight per day, well above the 0.8 g/kg RDA. A GLP-1-specific nutrition advisory issued by four medical societies recommends a more conservative 1.2 to 1.6 g/kg during active weight reduction. The guide includes a lookup table and three worked sample days.

Why is it so hard to eat enough protein on a GLP-1?

The medication reduces energy intake by roughly a quarter in controlled studies and quiets food-related thoughts, so protein is usually the first thing to fall. In one cross-sectional study, only about 43 percent of GLP-1 users met even a modest protein minimum. The fix is mechanical: eat on a schedule rather than waiting for hunger.

Do protein shakes work if I cannot eat solid food?

A shake delivers 20 to 30 grams of protein in a form that goes down on a low-appetite or nauseated day. In the protein supplementation meta-analysis, the protein source played a minor role while total daily intake was what mattered. Whey, casein, and plant proteins all support training gains.
